Purpose

This digital lending library is operated exclusively for the religious and educational purposes of the Oratory and its faithful. It exists to provide members with access to Catholic theological, spiritual, liturgical, and educational materials in support of the mission of the Church.

{# ── One-Copy, One-Loan ──────────────────────────────────────── #}

One-Copy, One-Loan Lending Model

This library follows a controlled digital lending model. For each physical or licensed copy of a work owned by the Oratory, only one digital loan may be active at any given time. When a copy is on loan to a patron, it is unavailable to other patrons until it is returned or the loan period expires. This mirrors the traditional lending practices of a physical library.

Each loaned copy is individually watermarked and time-limited. Upon expiration or return, the patron's access to the digital copy is revoked.

{# ── Restricted Access ───────────────────────────────────────── #}

Restricted Access

Access to this library is restricted to registered and approved patrons of the Oratory. The catalog and all digital materials are available only to authenticated members. No materials are made available to the general public. Registration requires approval by a librarian or administrator.

{# ── Takedown Requests ──────────────────────────────────────── #}

Takedown Requests

If you are a rights holder and believe that a work in this collection infringes on your rights, please contact us at {{ library_contact_email }}. We take all such requests seriously and will respond promptly.

Please include the title of the work in question, your relationship to the rights holder, and any supporting documentation.
