A Living Collection, Sustained by Generosity

The library of the Oratory exists for the spiritual and intellectual enrichment of its faithful. Every volume on our shelves—whether a patristic commentary, a manual of ascetical theology, or a children's catechism—is placed there so that it might form souls in the truth and beauty of the Catholic Faith.

Maintaining and growing a collection of this kind is a work of charity. Your generosity, whether through the gift of a book or a financial contribution, directly sustains this apostolate and ensures that the riches of Catholic thought remain accessible to every member of the Oratory.

{# ── Donate Books ──────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Donate Books

If you possess Catholic works that you believe would benefit the Oratory's collection—works of theology, Sacred Scripture, spirituality, Church history, canon law, liturgy, hagiography, or Catholic letters—we warmly welcome your donations. Books in good condition may be mailed to:

Library of the Oratory of the Most Sacred Hearts
PO Box 431521
Miami, FL 33243-1521

We are particularly grateful for works bearing an imprimatur or nihil obstat, editions of the Church Fathers, pre-conciliar manuals, and titles that have been requested by our patrons. If you are unsure whether a particular volume would be a good fit for the collection, please do not hesitate to contact us at {{ library_contact_email }} before sending.

{# ── Monetary Donations ────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Make a Financial Contribution

The digital library requires ongoing resources to operate: hosting costs, software maintenance, the digitization of physical volumes, and above all the acquisition of new titles to meet the needs of our patrons. A financial gift of any amount helps to ensure the continuity and growth of this work.

Monetary donations are used for:

  • Purchasing books requested by patrons of the Oratory
  • Acquiring new editions of essential Catholic texts
  • Covering the costs of digitization and preservation
  • Maintaining the infrastructure of the digital lending system

To make a financial contribution, please contact us at {{ library_contact_email }} for instructions. We are deeply grateful for every act of generosity, however modest.

{# ── Closing ───────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

A Word of Thanks

Every book donated, every contribution made, is an investment in the formation of Catholic souls. The Oratory remembers its benefactors in prayer. May God reward your generosity and may the saints whose words fill these pages intercede for you and your families.

“Each one must give as he has decided in his heart, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.”
— 2 Corinthians 9:7
